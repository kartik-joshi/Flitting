Description

Flitting is a platform that provides all the help one needs to move and settle into a new city or even a new country. All the help that is provided by Flitting is well investigated and is hence very trustworthy. Flitting is also very quick and easy to use. The move to a new location is made easier than one can expect. Flitting is a platform that provides a person all the help they need to move into a new place. Flitting has a list of people, in a location, who can help a person move in. All the people listed on the app will have a rating that is based on the feedback provided by other people who have been helped by this person.

The Flitting app has been developed with the aim of helping both users, who are relocating, the helpers and the local businesses. By enabling users to make use of the Flitting app to assist them in relocating, it can be ensured that the local businesses and local services that are provided, are used by the helpers to aid the users in relocating to the new location.

It is ensured that all the details present in the Flitting app, related to a contact, are always kept updated. Hence, the users of the Flitting app, need not worry about stale data being present in the app and can be convinced that they will get the best service present out there. There are contacts listed for everything that one might need to move in, to a new place.

Happy relocating!!!
